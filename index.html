What Data Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile (name, username, bio, profile photo)

  • Device and session information

Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.

Data Retention (Temporary)

For security, anti-fraud, and legal compliance, some information may be stored for up to 90 days after deletion request:

  • Abuse or safety reports
  • Security and system logs
  • Transaction or audit logs (if applicable)

After 90 days, all retained data is permanently deleted.

Partial Data Deletion

Wildslate does not support partial data deletion (such as deleting only messages or posts). You may still delete your posts manually inside the app.
